Drug Discovery

  • Paracetamol

    Paracetamol (acetaminophen[a]) is a non-opioid analgesic and antipyretic agent. It is widely used medication for relieving pain and reducing fever.

  • Levothyroxine

    Levothyroxine is a synthetic thyroid hormone. It is used to treat hypothyroidism by replacing or supplementing low thyroid hormone levels.

  • Metoprolol

    Metoprolol is a beta-blocker medication. It is used to manage high blood pressure, heart conditions, and to improve survival after heart attacks.
